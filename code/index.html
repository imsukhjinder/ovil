Induction Gold
Melting Furnace

Indiction Gold Meltrer

Induction technology is about heating and melting of ferrous and nonferrous metal through an inductive electromagnetic field. It consist of highly conductive copper tubing that is special wound into a Coil. The Induction power supply generates a high electrical current that travels to the coil through flexible water cooled power cables. An electromagentic field is created with in the center area of coil and reacts with metal placed in crucible. Due to high heat temperature the entire system is water cooled and water travels at the same route as the electricity.
